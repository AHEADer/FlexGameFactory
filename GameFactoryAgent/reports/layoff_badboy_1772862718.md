## Qualitative Assessment

Oh, *another* game trying to cash in on the ever-present dread of AI replacing everyone. As if real life isn't already a soul-crushing conveyor belt towards irrelevance, now we get to *simulate* it. The 'mechanics' are as exciting as a mandatory corporate training video: click button, numbers go up or down, conveyor moves you inevitably closer to the void. 'Upskill' gives measly points, 'Office Politics' is a transparent attempt to delay the inevitable, and 'Crunch Time' is just a glorified coin flip disguised as 'push your luck' – a tired trope for a tired premise. The 'AI Synergy Points' are meaningless, serving only as a counter until the predictable 'Game Over' message tells you what you already knew: you're disposable. The aesthetic is bland, the avatar is a lazy emoji, and the 'simulation' aspect is about as deep as a puddle. A thoroughly depressing and uninspired experience that offers nothing but a digital echo of real-world anxieties.

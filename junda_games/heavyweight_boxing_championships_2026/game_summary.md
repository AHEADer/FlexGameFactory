# Game Summary: Pyramid Punch-Out: Promoter War 2026

**Theme**: A satirical web game based on the 2026 Heavyweight Boxing explosion, specifically the "Glory in Giza" crossover and the escalating promoter feud between Eddie Hearn and Dana White.

**Chosen Game Mechanics**:
1.  **ACT-01 Action Points**: Used to manage your limited promotion energy per round.
2.  **AUC-04 Sealed-Bid Auction**: Bidding against rival promoters to sign top-tier fighters like Usyk, Verhoeven, and Mayweather.
3.  **UNC-02 Push-Your-Luck**: A high-stakes fight resolution system where you decide when to risk everything for a knockout.

**Core Loop**:
Players take the role of a cutthroat boxing promoter. Each "Round" starts with a bidding phase to sign a fighter using a sealed-bid system. Once signed, players spend Action Points to build "Hype" (increasing the potential payout) and "Stamina" (durability in the ring). The climax is the fight at the Pyramids of Giza, where players use a push-your-luck mechanic to throw increasingly risky combinations. Successful risks lead to massive payouts, but over-pushing causes a "Bust" (KO), losing the investment.

**Win/Loss Condition**:
*   **Win**: Accumulate $100 Million in profit from fight purses.
*   **Loss**: Go bankrupt (hit $0) or your star fighter's career is ended by 3 consecutive KOs.
